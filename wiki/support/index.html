Frequently Asked Questions

Why was my account banned?

Accounts are banned for violating community guidelines (spam, harassment, or repeated rule violations). If you believe this is a mistake, use the report form below to contact an admin.

Why is my post not showing up?

New posts are reviewed by an admin before publishing. Check your Account page under "My Posts" to see the current status (Pending, Published, Rejected).

How do I reset my password?

Go to the login page and click "Forgot password" to receive a reset link via email.

How do I report a user or a post?

Use the form below. Select the correct category and provide as much detail as possible so our admins can act quickly.
